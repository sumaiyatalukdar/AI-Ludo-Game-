# 🎲 AI-Integrated Ludo Game

An enhanced version of the classic Ludo board game powered by Artificial Intelligence and modern gameplay modes.

This project transforms the traditional Ludo experience into a smarter and more engaging game by integrating AI-based decision-making and introducing exciting new modes.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🤖 **AI Opponent**
  Play against an intelligent computer that makes strategic decisions instead of random moves.

* 👥 **Team Up Mode**
  Play in teams and collaborate to win the game.

* ⚡ **Rush Mode**
  A fast-paced version of Ludo for quick and intense gameplay.

* 🎮 **Classic Mode Enhanced**
  Traditional Ludo gameplay with improved UI and smoother experience.

---

## 🧠 AI Integration

The game incorporates AI to enhance gameplay by:

* Analyzing moves and making strategic decisions
* Simulating intelligent opponent behavior
* Improving game difficulty and engagement

AI-based Ludo implementations often focus on strategic decision-making and gameplay optimization to create a more challenging experience ([GitHub][1]).

## 🎯 How to Play

* Roll the dice and move your tokens
* Reach the home area before opponents
* Use strategy to defeat AI or other players

Ludo is a strategy-based board game where players race their tokens to the finish using dice rolls and tactical moves ([Wikipedia][2]).
